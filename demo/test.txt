      <h4>What is JHASIR (Joint Hearing Loss and Auditory System Injury Registry)?</h4>
      <p> Joint Hearing Loss and Auditory System Injury Registry (JHASIR) is  an app  that identifies and tracks personnel with hearing loss and  auditory/vestibular injuries. The app helps support readiness  through monitoring comprehensive hearing health programs, makes  available longitudinal data to clinical providers, and presents bidirectional data sharing between DOD and VA. </p>
